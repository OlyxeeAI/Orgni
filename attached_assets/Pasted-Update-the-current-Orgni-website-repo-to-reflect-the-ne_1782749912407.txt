Update the current Orgni website/repo to reflect the new product positioning.

Goal:
Orgni should be positioned as a product for “live business context,” not as a generic AI workflow, chatbot, or document assistant.

Core message:
“Live business context for modern operations.”

Product definition:
Orgni maps how a business works — its knowledge, processes, decisions, systems, and responsibilities — so intelligent tools can operate with the right context.

Company/product distinction:

* Olyxee is the company.
* Orgni is the product/platform.
* Olyxee builds research and infrastructure for operational intelligence.
* Orgni is Olyxee’s first product for live business context.

Your business changes faster than your systems understand.

Company knowledge lives across documents, chats, spreadsheets, tools, and people. Decisions are made every day, but the context behind them is rarely captured. Processes change, responsibilities shift, and teams lose operational memory.

Orgni creates a live context layer that keeps your business understandable as it operates.
