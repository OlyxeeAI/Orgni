**Orgni is the product.**
There should not be separate products called **Orgni Workflow**, **Orgni Finance**, **Orgni Controls**, etc.

Those should be described as **capabilities inside Orgni**, not separate products.

Use this structure:

| Level                         | Meaning                                          |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Olyxee**                    | Company                                          |
| **Orgni**                     | Product/platform                                 |
| **Capabilities inside Orgni** | Context, memory, decisions, operations, controls |

So on the website, say:

> **Orgni is Olyxee’s product for live business context.**

Not:

> “Orgni Context, Orgni Memory, Orgni Operations…”

Better wording:

### What Orgni does

Orgni helps organizations build live business context by connecting:

* company knowledge
* decisions
* processes
* systems
* responsibilities
* operating history
* permissions

### Capabilities

Use capability names, not product names:

| Capability     | Description                                                     |
