Create a premium landing page for **Orgni**, an AI infrastructure platform by Olyxee.

Orgni is not a chatbot, not an AI agent, and not a generic automation tool. Orgni is the business-context layer that learns how a company operates, then powers real workflows, finance operations, and future AI tools with context, control, and a clear trail.

The website must feel unique, simple, and enterprise-ready. It should be easy to understand without long reading.

Main goal:
Show that Orgni helps businesses move from scattered information to AI-enabled execution.

Core message:
**Orgni learns how your business works, then turns that understanding into usable infrastructure for workflows and finance.**

Design direction:

* Clean white background.
* Premium but simple.
* No clutter.
* No long text sections.
* No generic AI gradients.
* No chatbot-style UI.
* Use a strong sidebar or command-center inspired visual language.
* Make the page feel like a real product, not a demo.
* Use motion meaningfully, not decoratively.

Hero section:

* Big headline: **Business context for AI execution.**
* Short subtext: **Orgni learns your processes, rules, roles, documents, and exceptions so AI can support real business work.**
* Primary CTA: **Explore Orgni**
* Secondary CTA: **See how it works**
* Hero visual should show a live operating map: documents, roles, workflows, finance exceptions, approvals, and actions connecting into one Orgni core.

Interactive scrolling idea:
As the user scrolls, show Orgni “coming alive” step by step:

1. **Input**
   Show documents, spreadsheets, policies, voice notes, emails, and business descriptions entering Orgni.

2. **Understanding**
   Orgni extracts:

   * departments
   * roles
   * rules
   * workflows
   * source documents
   * approval paths
   * exceptions
   * missing information

3. **Business Map**
   Show a clean animated map of how the business operates.

4. **Orgni Workflows**
   Show operational workflows becoming active:

   * tasks
   * approvals
   * handovers
   * escalations
   * status updates
   * next actions

5. **Orgni Finance**
   Show finance intelligence:

   * bank statement
   * ledger
   * invoice
   * reconciliation
   * matched items
   * exceptions needing review
   * audit trail

6. **Execution**
   Show Orgni pushing clean context into workflows, finance tools, dashboards, and AI agents.

Landing page structure:

Section 1: Hero
Headline, short explanation, product visual.

Section 2: The Problem
Use short cards:

* Business knowledge is scattered.
* AI lacks operational context.
* Workflows depend on people’s memory.
* Finance exceptions take too long to resolve.
* Legacy systems are hard to connect.

Section 3: What Orgni Does
Keep it very simple:
**Orgni reads how your business works and turns it into structured intelligence.**

Show 4 outputs:

* Business map
* Workflow logic
* Finance context
* Audit trail

Section 4: How It Works
Use a horizontal or scroll-based animated flow:

Business inputs → Orgni understanding → Business map → Workflows + Finance → Clear execution

Section 5: Orgni Workflows
Show a live workflow panel:

* New request received
* Department identified
* Approval rule found
* Task assigned
* Escalation path ready
* Status updated

Section 6: Orgni Finance
Show a finance panel:

* Statement uploaded
* Ledger compared
* Matches confirmed
* Exceptions flagged
* Source evidence linked
* Review queue created

Section 7: Why Orgni Is Different
Short comparison:

* Not just search.
* Not just automation.
* Not just dashboards.
* Not just agents.
* Orgni gives AI the business context it needs to act safely.

Section 8: Built For
Show simple audience cards:

* Businesses adopting AI
* Finance teams
* Operations teams
* Companies on old systems
* Teams with scattered documents and processes

Section 9: Final CTA
Headline:
**Turn business knowledge into execution infrastructure.**

Buttons:

* **Start with Orgni**
* **View product flow**

Visual requirements:

* Use real product-like UI components.
* Use concise panels, not long paragraphs.
* Include micro-animations where data flows into Orgni.
* The scroll should feel like the system is waking up.
* Use clear labels, strong spacing, and restrained colors.
* Avoid fake metrics unless clearly illustrative.
* Make the page understandable in under 30 seconds.

Tone:
Clear, confident, minimal, enterprise-grade.

Do not use:

* Chatbot bubbles as the main visual.
* Overused AI brain imagery.
* Long marketing paragraphs.
* Purple/blue generic SaaS gradients.
* Buzzwords without showing what they mean.
* “AI agent platform” as the main positioning.

Final feeling:
The user should immediately understand that Orgni is the operating context layer for AI-enabled business execution.
