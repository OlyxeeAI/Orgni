Finance Operations Workflow — Rapid Freight Solutions

INVOICE APPROVAL PROCESS:
1. Admin receives supplier invoice via email
2. Finance Assistant reviews invoice (supplier name, amount, date, line items)
3. Finance Assistant matches invoice against delivery records in the delivery log spreadsheet
4. If invoice amount exceeds R5,000, Finance Manager approval is required before payment
5. If no matching delivery record is found, invoice is flagged and held — supplier is notified
6. Approved invoices are submitted to Finance Manager for payment scheduling

BANK RECONCILIATION (MONTH-END):
1. Finance Assistant downloads bank statement at end of month
2. Transactions are manually matched against the general ledger in Excel
3. Unmatched items are listed as exceptions and reviewed by Finance Manager
4. Reconciliation report is prepared and signed off by Finance Manager
5. Any discrepancies above R10,000 are escalated to Director

PAYMENT APPROVAL RULES:
- Payments under R5,000: Finance Assistant can approve
- Payments R5,000 to R50,000: Finance Manager approval required
- Payments above R50,000: Director sign-off required
- All international payments: Director sign-off required regardless of amount

EXCEPTION HANDLING:
- Missing delivery record: Flag invoice, notify supplier, hold payment
- Duplicate invoice: Reject and notify supplier
- Amount mismatch vs purchase order: Hold and escalate to operations
- Unrecognised supplier: Escalate to Finance Manager for verification
